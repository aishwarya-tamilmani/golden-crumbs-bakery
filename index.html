Our Story

A Little Kitchen, A Lot of Heart.

Golden Crumbs began in a small home kitchen with one simple belief — the best cakes come from real ingredients and real care. Today, every order is still hand-mixed, hand-piped and hand-delivered, just the way it started. No shortcuts, no factory lines — only fresh butter, real cream, and a design made specially for your celebration.
